The BSc Chemistry program at ABC Arts and Science College offers a comprehensive education in the principles and applications of chemistry. The curriculum encompasses organic, inorganic, physical, and analytical chemistry, along with biochemistry, providing students with a solid foundation in chemical sciences. Equipped with modern laboratories and state-of-the-art instruments, the program emphasizes practical skills and hands-on experience through extensive laboratory work and research projects. Guided by a team of experienced faculty members, students are encouraged to engage in scientific inquiry and innovation. Graduates of the BSc Chemistry program are well-prepared for careers in chemical industries, research and development, pharmaceuticals, environmental science, and education, as well as for pursuing advanced studies and specialized professional courses.
