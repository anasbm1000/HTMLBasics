The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program at ABC Arts and Science College offers a dynamic curriculum designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science and information technology. This program covers essential topics such as programming languages, database management, software engineering, web development, and networking. Students benefit from state-of-the-art computer labs, experienced faculty, and hands-on projects that emphasize practical skills and real-world applications. With a focus on both theoretical knowledge and practical expertise, the BCA program prepares graduates for successful careers in the IT industry and equips them with the necessary skills for further studies and professional certifications.
