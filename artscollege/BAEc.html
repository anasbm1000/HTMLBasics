The BA Economics program at ABC Arts and Science College offers a robust and comprehensive education in economic theories, principles, and applications. The curriculum covers microeconomics, macroeconomics, econometrics, development economics, and international economics, providing students with a thorough understanding of economic analysis and policy-making. The program is designed to develop strong analytical and quantitative skills, facilitated by experienced faculty and state-of-the-art resources. Through a combination of theoretical knowledge and practical insights, including case studies and research projects, students are well-prepared for careers in finance, government, business, and academia, as well as for advanced studies in economics and related fields.
