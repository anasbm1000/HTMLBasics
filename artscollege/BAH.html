The BA History program at ABC Arts and Science College is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of historical events, cultures, and societies from ancient to modern times. Through a diverse curriculum that includes courses on world history, regional studies, historiography, and thematic studies such as economic, social, and political history, students develop critical thinking and analytical skills. The program is supported by a team of experienced faculty and access to extensive resources, including archives and digital databases. Emphasizing both academic rigor and research, the BA History program prepares students for careers in education, research, public service, and various roles in cultural and heritage organizations, as well as laying the groundwork for further academic pursuits.
