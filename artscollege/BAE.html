The BA English program at ABC Arts and Science College is meticulously designed to immerse students in the rich tapestry of English literature, language, and critical theory. This program provides a robust foundation through core courses in British, American, and World Literature, along with modules on Literary Theory and Language Studies. Students have the opportunity to enhance their skills with electives in Creative Writing and Film Studies, promoting both analytical and creative prowess. With a distinguished faculty, state-of-the-art resources, and a vibrant academic environment, the BA English program aims to develop proficient communicators, critical thinkers, and culturally aware individuals ready for diverse career opportunities and advanced studies.
